# HQTiers

A client-side Fabric mod that brings your PvPHQ stats in-game and shows nametags, tab list, leaderboards and more. Fully customizable via **Mod Menu**.

## Features

**Stats**
- PvPHQ stats on player nametags and the tab list
- Global, Highest Tier, and per-ladder (all gamemodes) display modes
- Choose which stats show and in what order (look in Mod Menu under Nametag)

**Leaderboards & Profiles**
- In-game leaderboard browser, scroll pages, switch ladders, click to open profiles
- Search players by name
- Revisit recently viewed players and save favorites
- View ladder stats, positions, and rating history
- Copy a Discord-ready stats summary or shareable profile link

**Other**
- `/hqtiers <player>`: look up any player's ranked stats

## Requirements
[Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) · [Cloth Config](https://modrinth.com/mod/cloth-config) · [Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu)
